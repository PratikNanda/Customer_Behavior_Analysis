# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end Data Analytics project using **Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, and Power BI** to analyze 3,900 shopping transactions and deliver actionable business insights.

---

## Project Summary

This project explores customer behavior, revenue drivers, and product performance to help businesses improve marketing, retention, and profitability.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **Python** – Data cleaning & feature engineering  
- **PostgreSQL / SQL** – Business analysis & querying  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard & visualization

---

## Workflow

1. **Data Preparation (Python)**
   - Cleaned 3,900 records  
   - Handled missing values  
   - Created age groups & purchase frequency metrics  

2. **Business Analysis (SQL)**
   - Revenue by gender & age group  
   - Customer segmentation (New / Returning / Loyal)  
   - Discount dependency analysis  
   - Subscription & repeat buyer behavior  

3. **Visualization (Power BI)**
   - KPI dashboard  
   - Customer segments  
   - Product performance  
   - Revenue insights

---

## Key Insights

- **Male customers generated 2× revenue**: $157,890 vs $75,191  
- **Young adults** drive highest revenue (~$62K)  
- **Hats** show ~50% discount dependency  
- **3,116 customers are loyal**  
- **Subscribers & repeat buyers spend more**

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen loyalty & subscription programs  
- Reduce over-discounting on key products  
- Target high-value age segments  
- Promote top-rated products

---

## Outcome

A complete analytics pipeline transforming raw data into business decisions.
